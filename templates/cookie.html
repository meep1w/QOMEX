Cookie Policy

Last updated May 19, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how QOMEXtrading ("Company," "we," "us," and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at http://www.qomex.top ("Website").

It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

In some cases we may use cookies to collect personal information, or that becomes personal information if we combine it with other information.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently.

"First-party cookies" are set by us. "Third-party cookies" are set by other parties and enable third-party functionality like analytics or ads.

Why do we use cookies?

We use both first- and third-party cookies for various reasons:

  • Essential cookies – required for our Website to function.
  • Analytics and targeting cookies – to understand and improve user experience.
  • Third-party cookies – used for ads, analytics, and personalization.

How can I control cookies?

You can accept or reject cookies using the Cookie Consent Manager on our Website. Essential cookies cannot be rejected.

You may also change browser settings to control cookies. Disabling cookies may limit certain features.

Types of cookies we use

The cookies listed below may vary depending on your interaction with our Website:

  • _ga_# – Distinguishes users for session tracking.
    Expires in: 1 year 1 month 4 days
  • _ga – Stores user-specific usage data.
    Expires in: 1 year 1 month 4 days

What about other tracking technologies?

We may also use web beacons (or "tracking pixels") that help us understand user behavior and track email open rates. These often rely on cookies.

Do you use Flash cookies or Local Shared Objects?

We may use Flash cookies for fraud prevention and service functionality. You can manage Flash settings via Adobe's settings panels:

  • Website Storage Settings Panel
  • Global Storage Settings Panel

Blocking Flash cookies may impair some functionality.

Do you serve targeted advertising?

Yes, third-party cookies may deliver ads relevant to your interests. These cookies do not collect identifying info unless you choose to provide it.
